Welcome to MindSheet

Your personal mind-map canvas. Everything is saved on this device instantly and synced to your own Google Sheet in the background.

double-click / double-tap anywhere — create a note
drag empty space — pan · pinch / Ctrl+scroll — zoom
select a note, then drag its round dot onto another — link them

To sync across laptop and phone, connect your Google Sheet in settings (takes ~5 min, steps in SETUP.md).
